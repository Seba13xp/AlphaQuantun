Quantum Computers

The Age of Computing

Quantum computing is a technological breakthrough which allows for the creation of computers that are thousands of times faster than their standard counterparts. Quantum computing changes the field of computer science. By using quantum physics to read data in multiples states, quantum computers can read and interpret data at astonishing speeds. Quantum computers will lead to many new and improved uses for computers, thanks to their ability to process data at incredible speeds.
